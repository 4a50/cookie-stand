Meet Pat and Family

Pat grew up in a small town just outside of the Seattle area. He was always welcomed home for a fun day of fishing on the weekends with the smell of warm baked cookies. As Pat grew up he discoverd his passion for baking, as well as his love of catching salmon. One day at the dinner table Pat set down his fork, looked at his family and said, "Salmon Cookies!". The rest is history!

Salmon Cookies you say!?

That's right, Salmon Cookies! These are a one of a kind treat you are sure to love! Made from a secret recipe developed of 10 years by Pat and his family. We know, the combination of Salmon and Cookies doesn't sound that appetizing, but once you wrap your lips around these, you taste buds will scream for more. Boy do we have more! Salmon Cookies are availible all over the world, and you can only get them is store! Amazing!
